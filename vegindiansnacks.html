Pakora

Ingredients: Gram Flour, Spinach, Potatoes, Cauliflower, Spices

A savory snack made with gram flour and vegetables like spinach, potatoes, or cauliflower, deep-fried to a crisp.

Samosa

Ingredients: Potatoes, Peas, Spices, Pastry Dough

A popular snack consisting of a crispy pastry shell filled with spiced mashed potatoes, peas, and herbs.

Pani Puri

Ingredients: Puri, Potatoes, Chickpeas, Spicy Water

A delicious street food made by stuffing crispy puris with spicy water, potatoes, and chickpeas.

Bhel Puri

Ingredients: Puffed Rice, Sev, Onions, Tomatoes, Chutneys

A savory snack made from puffed rice, sev, onions, tomatoes, and sweet and tangy chutneys.

Dhokla

Ingredients: Rice Flour, Chickpea Flour, Yogurt, Spices

A steamed, spongy savory cake made with fermented rice and chickpea flour, often served with chutney.
