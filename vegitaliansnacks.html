Arancini

Ingredients: Arborio Rice, Cheese, Breadcrumbs, Marinara Sauce

Arancini are golden, crispy rice balls stuffed with cheese, peas, or mushrooms, and served with marinara sauce for dipping.

Crostini

Ingredients: Bread, Tomato, Olive Tapenade, Cheese

Crostini are small slices of toasted bread served with toppings like tomato, olive tapenade, or creamy cheese, making for a delicious snack.

Supplì

Ingredients: Arborio Rice, Mozzarella Cheese, Breadcrumbs

Supplì are similar to arancini, but with mozzarella cheese in the center. They are deep-fried to golden perfection and often served with marinara sauce.

Bruschetta

Ingredients: Bread, Tomato, Garlic, Basil, Olive Oil

Bruschetta is a popular Italian snack made with toasted bread topped with fresh tomatoes, garlic, basil, and a drizzle of olive oil.

Frittata

Ingredients: Eggs, Cheese, Vegetables, Olive Oil

A frittata is a versatile Italian dish made with eggs, cheese, and vegetables, and can be served warm or at room temperature as a snack.
