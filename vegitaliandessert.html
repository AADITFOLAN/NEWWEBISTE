Tiramisu

Ingredients: Ladyfingers, Espresso, Mascarpone, Cocoa Powder

A beloved Italian dessert, Tiramisu consists of layers of coffee-soaked ladyfingers, rich mascarpone cream, and cocoa powder.

Cannoli

Ingredients: Pastry Shells, Ricotta Cheese, Chocolate Chips, Candied Fruits

Cannoli are crispy tubes of pastry filled with a sweet ricotta cheese mixture, often studded with chocolate chips or candied fruit.

Panna Cotta

Ingredients: Cream, Sugar, Vanilla, Gelatin

Panna Cotta is a velvety Italian dessert made from cream, sugar, and vanilla, often served with a fruit coulis or caramel sauce.

Baba al Rum

Ingredients: Flour, Yeast, Sugar, Rum Syrup

Baba al Rum are small yeast cakes soaked in rum syrup, often served as a sweet indulgence after a meal.

Zabaglione

Ingredients: Egg Yolks, Sugar, Marsala Wine

Zabaglione is a silky custard dessert made with egg yolks, sugar, and Marsala wine, often served warm or cold with fruit or cake.
